math.frandom (low, high)
Works like math.random(low,high), but returns a float instead of an int.

Parameters:

  • low number or nil (Optional) The lower bound of the number.
  • high number or nil (Optional) The upper bound of the number.

Returns:

    number A random number in the range (low,high) if low and high are provided, in (0,low) if low is provided, or in (0,1) if neither are.
